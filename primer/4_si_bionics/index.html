Standard Issue Bionics

There may be no more Internet granting access to the world's knowledge at light speed but in today's cataclysmic world, your little SI Bonic is just about as good. Your SI provides you with a lot information regarding your current situation and we'll be going over its many features in case you've forgotten.

NOTE:

The "Standard Issue Bionic" is not a real game feature, but a metaphor for explaining the game's user-interface from the perspective of an in-game character.

Renderer

Featuring the most real-estate is the Renderer which is going to display a picture of what is around you right now. You are the little @ in the middle. As you move around, the renderer will update to reflect your new surroundings. By pressing the x key you can move a cursor around the display causing the SI to identify any objects in that location.
